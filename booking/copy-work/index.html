Copy Work

Clean, color-conscious artwork documentation for artists, galleries, makers, and collectors.

Copy work is for artists who need accurate documentation of paintings, drawings, prints, and other flat artwork for portfolios, archives, websites, juried submissions, and reproduction files.

Artwork Documentation

Photographs that keep the artwork honest.

The goal is simple: clean lighting, controlled perspective, color-accuracy, and files that represent the work clearly. Your art stays the main character.

Process

How it works

Send the details

Tell me how many pieces you have, the approximate sizes, whether they are framed, and what the final files are for.

We plan the setup

We’ll decide whether the work should be photographed at your studio, another location, or in a controlled setup.

I photograph and prepare files

Artwork is photographed with attention to alignment, light, surface glare, and practical color accuracy.

You receive organized files

Final images are delivered clearly labeled and ready for your intended use.
